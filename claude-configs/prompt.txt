- get_stock_info

- calculate_pead_score
- get_historical_stock_prices
- get_stock_actions

After iterating through each stock give only following information and nothing else

1. A table with columns, company name, PEAD score, Result Date, Forward PE, Sales YoY, Sales QoQ, NP YoY, NP QoQ, EBIDT YoY, EBIDT QoQ, CF/Profit
2. Technical analysis including current price, price change, 52W high/low, RSI, MACD and Overall technical signal
3. News sentiment including overall sentiment, positive news, negative news
4. Fundamental analysis including P/E Ratio, P/B Ratio, Market Cap, Profit Margin, Debt/Equity Ratio, Dividend Yield
5. Conclusion which has the details recommendations for given stocks.
